Privacy Policy

FaceMetric AI
Effective Date: March 21, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 25, 2026

FaceMetric AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and handle your data — specifically face data — when you use the FaceMetric AI mobile application (the "App").

📷 1. Collection of Face Data

When you use FaceMetric AI, you voluntarily provide a single facial photograph via your device's photo library or camera. This photograph constitutes the only "face data" we collect.

We do NOT collect, use, or access:
  • TrueDepth camera data or depth maps
  • ARKit face tracking data
  • Face ID or any biometric identifier data
  • Any facial recognition or identification data

The face data we receive is limited exclusively to the standard 2D photograph you provide.

🧠 2. Use of Face Data

Your face data is used solely and exclusively for the following purpose:

  • To perform an Aesthetic Profilometric Analysis, which calculates facial symmetry, geometric proportions, and aesthetic ratios based on the photograph you submit.
Your face data is NOT used for:
  • User identification or authentication
  • Facial recognition
  • Marketing, advertising, or user profiling
  • Training machine learning models
  • Any purpose other than delivering your requested analysis report

🗑️ 3. Face Data Retention — Explicit Statement

Face data is NOT retained. We explicitly confirm that FaceMetric AI does not retain, store, or archive any face data after the analysis is complete.

Complete lifecycle of your face data:

1
Upload: Your photograph is transmitted securely via HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) to our dedicated analysis server.
2
Processing: The photograph is held in volatile (temporary) server memory only for the duration required to complete the AI-powered aesthetic analysis. This processing time is typically less than 60 seconds.
3
Immediate Deletion: Upon completion of the analysis and delivery of the report to your device, all face data — including the original photograph and any processed derivatives — is immediately and permanently deleted from our server's memory. No copies are saved to disk, database, or any persistent storage medium.

Reason We Do NOT Store Face Data

Face data is not required beyond the real-time analysis session. Since the sole purpose of collecting your photograph is to generate an immediate analysis report, there is no technical or business need to retain the data after the report is delivered. We have designed our system architecture to ensure face data exists only in temporary memory during active processing.

Duration of Storage

Your face data exists on our server for a maximum of 60 seconds (the typical processing window). This brief duration is necessary because the AI model requires the photograph in memory to perform the geometric and symmetry calculations. Once the calculations are complete and the report is transmitted back to your device, the data is purged from memory immediately.

Why This Specific Duration

The 60-second window represents the maximum time needed for our AI model to analyze facial landmarks, calculate proportions, and generate the comprehensive report. This duration reflects the computational requirements of the analysis and nothing more. We do not extend this window for any other purpose.

🚫 4. Third-Party Sharing of Face Data

FaceMetric AI does NOT share your face data with any third parties.

Specifically:

  • We do not share face data with advertising networks or data brokers.
  • We do not share face data with analytics providers.
  • We do not share face data with social media platforms.
  • We do not share face data with any external companies, organizations, or individuals.
  • We do not share face data with any AI model training services or cloud AI platforms.

Reason We Do Not Share

All analysis processing is performed entirely on our own dedicated server infrastructure. There is no technical or business reason to transmit your face data to any third party. Our system is self-contained and does not rely on external services for facial analysis.

Third-Party Storage

Since we do not share face data with any third parties, no third parties store your face data. There are zero third-party entities that receive, process, or retain your face data at any point.

🔒 5. Data Security

We employ the following security measures to protect your face data during the brief processing window:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmission between your device and our server is encrypted using HTTPS with TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Dedicated Server: Analysis is performed on our own dedicated server infrastructure. We do not use shared or multi-tenant cloud environments for face data processing.
  • No Persistent Storage: Our server architecture is designed so that face data is never written to disk or any persistent storage medium. Data exists solely in volatile (RAM) memory during processing.
  • Immediate Purge: Upon delivery of the analysis report, all face data in memory is immediately purged.

↩️ 6. Data Retention/Deletion Policy and Consent Revocation

Data Retention Policy

No face data is retained after the analysis is complete. All face data — including the original photograph and any processed derivatives — is immediately and permanently deleted from server memory upon completion of the analysis. Face data is never written to persistent storage (disk, database, or other storage medium) at any point during the process.

Data Deletion

Since face data is automatically and immediately deleted after each analysis session, there is no stored face data for us to provide, modify, or delete upon request once your analysis session ends. The analysis report generated from your photograph is stored only on your local device and can be deleted at any time by you.

How to delete your data:
  • Delete the analysis report from within the app
  • Delete the app from your device (which removes all locally stored data)
  • For any data deletion requests or concerns, contact us at info@idakimi.com

Consent Revocation

You may revoke your consent for face data collection at any time by simply choosing not to submit a photograph. The app presents a clear data usage consent prompt before your first analysis, explaining exactly how your data will be used. You may:

  • Before analysis: Choose whether to submit a photograph. The app does not capture photographs without your explicit action.
  • During analysis: Close the app at any time, and any in-progress data will be discarded.
  • After analysis: The analysis report is stored only on your local device. You may delete it at any time by deleting the app or its data.

If you have previously consented and wish to withdraw consent, you can simply stop using the analysis feature. No action is required to revoke consent as no data is retained between sessions.

⚕️ 7. Medical Disclaimer

FaceMetric AI is an aesthetic analysis tool designed exclusively for symmetry and geometric proportion evaluation. It does NOT provide:

  • Medical diagnoses
  • Clinical advice
  • Treatment recommendations
  • Health assessments

The analysis results are for informational and educational purposes only and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical consultation.

👶 8. Children's Privacy

FaceMetric AI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect face data or any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at info@idakimi.com.
